Welcome to Sabala

A Blend of Rustic Charm & Modern Comfort

Sabala Heritage Home is a unique homestay featuring stunning rustic stony architecture, surrounded by lush gardens and coconut trees. Spread across a two-acre property, we offer a peaceful retreat from the noise of the city.

While you immerse yourself in nature, we ensure you stay connected with modern amenities like high-speed WiFi. It's the perfect escape for those who appreciate authenticity without compromising on comfort.
